# Privacy Policy for Nova Solar Weather

**Last Updated:** November 25, 2025

## 1. Introduction

Nova Solar Weather (We) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles information.

## 2. Information We Do Not Collect

Our application does **NOT** collect, store, or share any personal information including but not limited to:
- Personal identification information
- Contact details
- Device information
- Usage statistics
- Any other sensitive data

## 3. Location Data

The app may request location permissions **only** to:
- Provide accurate local weather information
- Show relevant solar weather data for your area

**Important:** Your location data is processed locally on your device and is **never** transmitted to our servers or stored externally.

## 4. Data Storage

All user preferences and settings are stored locally on your device using:
- Local device storage
- Shared preferences

This data remains on your device and is not synchronized with any external servers.

## 5. Third-Party Services

Our app may use weather data from external APIs to provide accurate information. These services receive only anonymous, non-personal data necessary for weather functionality.

## 6. Children's Privacy

Our app does not specifically target children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

## 8. Your Consent

By using our application, you consent to this Privacy Policy.
